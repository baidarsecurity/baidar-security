Detailed Information & Policies

HR Policy

Staff is committed for providing each Client with optimum quality of protective services. With our exceptional security personnel, BAIDAR Security Services (Pvt.) Ltd. sets the industry standard for excellence. We recruit high caliber, professional, intelligent and dedicated personnel to meet the expectations of our clients. Our rigorous selection process ensures only the best join our team.

Most of BAIDAR Security Services (Pvt.) Ltd. guards belong to the Armed Forces and their selection and maintenance is dependent upon their physical appearance, health, age, duration of service in the armed forces and character. This ensures a foundation of discipline and integrity in our workforce.

Recruitment is carried out from retired army personnel. However, retired personnel from PAF, NAVY, FC, Rangers, and ASF may also be enrolled. Recruitment of experienced civilians as Security Guards is also undertaken. Educational qualification of civilian guards is generally Matric. We enroll security personnel of Category A+ (mentally and physically) with clean Police record, signifying our commitment to elite standards.

Verification Policy

We implement a stringent three-pronged verification process which is embedded in our HR policy, ensuring maximum trustworthiness and reliability. We strictly implement 100% NADRA (National Database Registration Authority) verification, Police security check for criminal record, and Local Administration verification. This comprehensive approach safeguards our clients from any potential risks associated with personnel.

Training

Baidar Security conducts training that exceeds that of any of our competitor’s programs, reflecting our dedication to continuous professional development. The extensive knowledge of our management team is meticulously passed on to our new employees through structured modules. In addition to the national mandated certification courses, our Security personnel also attend:

  • Defensive Tactics Training
  • Patrol Techniques & Criminal Law
  • Customer Service Seminar

In addition, apart from our own training program, we, at BAIDAR provides on-job training and specialized training such as Security and Protection Courses from the Elite Police Training School of Elite Punjab Police which includes handling and firing of 12 Bore Repeater and Pistol as well as Basic Unarmed Combat Techniques. Every security guard gets trained from the Police Training institute, Police Line, H-11, Islamabad having three continuous days of overall training of weapon handling, firing practice, ethics of security duty and unarmed combat techniques, ensuring they are fully prepared for any scenario.

On-the-job training is also carried out by the training instructors of Army Welfare Trust (Askari Guards) Periodically and by our own seasoned security staff, guaranteeing practical application and reinforcement of learned skills.

Armed Guards & Unarmed Guards

Based on security need analysis, when presence alone and the possibility of physical force may not be enough, well-trained armed guards with Military background who are expert and certified in effective use of firearm will be assigned to your location to ensure that your security needs are met to your satisfaction. Deploying armed guards is often the imperative need of the day for robust business protection. Unarmed security guards are mostly deployed at search points to check vehicles, visitors and employees, providing essential access control, but they are always covered and backed by an Armed Guard for ultimate safety. Our management team will work closely with you to determine which type of guarding service best suits your organizational security needs, offering tailored solutions.

Monitoring and Evaluation

The M&E framework ensures that the guards are providing services by creating a safe and secure environment for the clients, and this is meticulously measured by key indicators such as client satisfaction and performance. The guards are duly rewarded for their acts of valor and good performance, fostering a culture of excellence and motivation within our ranks. This recognition motivates the guards in performing their tough tasks more effectively and efficiently, contributing to overall operational success.

On the ground, duty supervisors meticulously check the performance of the guards at different locations on a daily basis and report back promptly to the Manager Operations, ensuring continuous oversight. In addition, surprise checks are conducted randomly to inspect the uniforms, weapons, turn out, and performance of guards, upholding our stringent standards. The margin of error in safety and security is minimal; therefore, appropriate corrective action is taken for any violation, maintaining absolute adherence to protocols. Crucial feedback from clients is regularly taken on a monthly basis, allowing us to adapt and refine our services continuously.
